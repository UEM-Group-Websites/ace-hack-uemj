Who are we and what is AceHack and ACM?

AceHack is a hackathon , an iniciative of UEM Jaipur ACM Student Chapter and which is being held in UEM Jaipur, India. It is a half-day 12 Hour event where participants can build their own projects and compete with other participants. The event is presided by the team of AceHack.

The mission of the AceHack 2022 hackathon is to solve real world problems and getting hands on experience.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where individuals can learn more about computer science or programming and enhance their skills by creating unique projects and attending workshops. At Web3Hack, students will be creating projects and competing with each other in order to win prizes and receive recognition.

Who can attend?

Anyone who can solve the problem statements.

When and where is the Hackathon?

AceHack will be held in UEM Jaipur Offline Mode & 14th September, 2022.

What is the vision of AceHack ?

The vision of AceHack is to empower hacker community and to motivate students about real world problem solving.
